Privacy Policy for SatChaser.org

Effective Date: 25 April, 2024

Welcome to SatChaser.org. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, process, and safeguard your personal information when you interact with our website and services.

Information We Collect

To provide you with our satellite tracking services, we collect the following information:

    Phone Number: We require your phone number to send you text messages containing satellite information and to confirm your account.
    Zip Code: Your zip code is used to determine the satellites that are currently overhead in your location.

How We Use Your Information

To Provide Services: Your information is used to deliver the services you request, such as sending you text messages with satellite data.

To Confirm Ownership: We send a confirmation code to your phone number to verify your identity and prevent fraud.

Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your information. All data you provide is encrypted and securely stored.

Third Parties

We use Twilio, a third-party service provider, to send text messages. Although Twilio has access to the data necessary to perform these tasks, they are obligated to protect your information and not use it for any other purposes.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights:

    Opt-out: You can send a text message saying "STOP" or "UNSUBSCRIBE" at any time to delete your profile.
    Access and Updates: You can request access to and update your personal information by contacting us at [contact information].

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide you with our services and as required by applicable law. If you unsubscribe or delete your profile, your information will be deleted promptly.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time in response to changing legal, technical, or business developments. When we update our policy, we will take appropriate measures to inform you, consistent with the significance of the changes we make.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our use of your personal information, please contact us me me@alexchaves.org. By using our services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this privacy policy. Thank you for choosing SatChaser.org.
